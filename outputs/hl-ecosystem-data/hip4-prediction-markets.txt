Hyperliquid’s **HIP-4 (Hyperliquid Improvement Proposal 4)**, launched in May 2026, introduces **Outcome Trading**—a native primitive for prediction markets built directly on the HyperCore L1. It is designed not as a standalone app, but as a "Lego piece" that integrates prediction markets into the same high-performance order book and margin system as Hyperliquid’s perpetuals and spot markets.

### **1. Mechanical Details: How it Works**
HIP-4 treats "Outcome Contracts" as fully collateralized binary instruments that settle between **0 and 1 USDC**.

*   **Merged Order Books:** This is the core innovation. The order books for "YES" and "NO" are mathematically linked. A buy order for "YES" at $0.60 is automatically treated as a sell order for "NO" at $0.40. This prevents liquidity fragmentation.
*   **Split, Merge, and Negate:** 
    *   **Split:** Users can "split" 1 USDC into one YES token and one NO token.
    *   **Merge:** Users can redeem one YES and one NO token back for 1 USDC.
    *   **Negate:** In multi-outcome markets (e.g., a 3-way race), holding a "NO" on every outcome is equivalent to holding the collateral, allowing for early redemption.
*   **1x Isolated Margin:** Unlike perps, outcome contracts are **fully collateralized**. There is no leverage, no liquidation risk, and no funding rates.
*   **Portfolio Margin:** While the contracts themselves aren't leveraged, your outcome positions sit in the same account as your perps. Their value can contribute to your overall margin, allowing for sophisticated cross-product hedging (e.g., using a prediction market to hedge a 50x long position).

### **2. Revenue Model & Ecosystem**
Hyperliquid's model is designed to drive value back to the **HYPE** token and the builders.

*   **Builder Staking:** To deploy a permissionless outcome market, a builder must stake **1,000,000 HYPE**. This stake is slashable if the builder manipulates the oracle, acting as a high-integrity barrier to entry.
*   **Fee Structure:** 
    *   **Zero Open Fees:** To incentivize trading, there are **0% fees to open** a position. 
    *   **Settlement/Close Fees:** Fees are charged only when closing a trade or at final settlement.
    *   **HYPE Burn:** 97% of protocol-wide trading fees (including perps and outcomes) are used to **buy back and burn HYPE**, creating a deflationary flywheel.
*   **Treasury Revenue:** The protocol generates an estimated **$160M/year** from the interest on the USDC collateral held in the margin system, which flows back to the HYPE ecosystem.
