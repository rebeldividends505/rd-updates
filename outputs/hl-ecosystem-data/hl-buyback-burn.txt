The **Hyperliquid Assistance Fund (AF)** is the core protocol-level engine responsible for the deflationary mechanics of the $HYPE token. It functions as an automated buyback-and-burn mechanism that converts protocol revenue into $HYPE and removes it from circulation permanently.

### **1. What is the Assistance Fund?**
The Assistance Fund is a system address (`0xfefefefefefefefefefefefefefefefefefefefe`) that acts as a "black hole." It was created without a private key, meaning any tokens sent to it are fundamentally inaccessible. 
*   **Funding:** It is primarily funded by **99% of all perpetual trading fees** (which moved from 97% to 99% in late 2025). 
*   **Mechanism:** The protocol's L1 execution layer automatically market-buys $HYPE using these fees and sends them to the AF address.
*   **Governance:** While technically "locked," a community vote formally recognized these tokens as "burned," removing them from the protocol's official total supply.

### **4. Comparison to Other Deflationary Assets**
Hyperliquid's burn mechanism is unique because it directs nearly **100% of its primary revenue** toward token scarcity, whereas most other protocols split revenue between treasuries, developers, or stakers.
