### **5. Wallets, Bridges & Tools**
*   **Wallets:** **Rabby** is the widely cited favorite for power users due to its native HyperCore signing support. **Best Wallet** and **Phantom** also provide deep ecosystem integration.
*   **Bridges:** 
    *   **Native Bridge:** Official route from Arbitrum (USDC).
    *   **Across Protocol:** Fast, intent-based bridging from 20+ chains.
    *   **deBridge:** Sub-second transfers between Solana and Hyperliquid.
*   **Aggregators:** **Jupiter** and **Rage Trade** route significant volume through Hyperliquid's deep liquidity pools.

### **6. Emerging Categories (NFTs, Gaming, AI)**
*   **NFTs:** **OmniX** is the primary omnichain NFT hub. **Hypurr** remains the most recognizable community badge/collection.
*   **Gaming:** **Meme Punch** (PvP arena) and **Yushi Miner** (strategy) are the leading on-chain games leveraging the L1's sub-second finality.
*   **AI Agents:** **Katoshi AI** and **Hyperbot** allow users to deploy autonomous trading agents that execute natural language commands directly on the order book.
*   **RWA:** **Midas RWA** ($2.4M TVL) and **Trade[XYZ]** provide tokenized treasury bills and S&P 500 perpetuals.
