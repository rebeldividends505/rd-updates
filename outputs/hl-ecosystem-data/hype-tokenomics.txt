### **Staking and Yield**
*   **Staking Ratio:** **45.15%** of the eligible supply is currently staked (~114.9M HYPE).
*   **Staking APY:** Approximately **2.25% – 2.3%**. Note that rewards are funded by a dedicated *Future Emissions Reserve* rather than token inflation.
*   **Lockup Period:** 1-day cooldown to unstake; ~7 days to transfer for trading.
*   **Utility:** Used for L1 gas fees, PoS security, governance, and tiered trading fee discounts on the Hyperliquid exchange.

### **Treasury and Wallets**
*   **Treasury (Hyper Foundation):** Manages the **6% Foundation budget** and the **38.89% Future Emissions** reserve. The Foundation has historically used its budget for "Assistance Fund" buybacks and strategic burns via governance.
*   **Community Wallet:** The remaining community-facing allocations (Grants and Emissions) are held for long-term ecosystem distribution.
*   **Team Vesting:** Team tokens are currently in a monthly release phase that began in January 2026 to ensure long-term alignment.
