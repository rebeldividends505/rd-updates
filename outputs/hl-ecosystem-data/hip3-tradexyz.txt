### 2. Asset List (RWAs & Traditional Markets)
Trade.xyz specializes in bringing TradFi liquidity to DeFi. Assets are traded as "Perpetual Futures," meaning you can go long or short with leverage without owning the underlying stock.

*   **Stocks:** High-liquidity US equities including **NVDA, AAPL, AMZN, AMD, MSFT,** and **TSLA**.
*   **Indices:** **XYZ100** (a native index of top 100 non-financial companies, similar to the Nasdaq-100) and **SP500**.
*   **Commodities:** **Gold (GOLD), Silver (SILVER), WTI Oil (WTIOIL),** and **Brent Crude**.
*   **Pre-IPO Markets:** Unique access to speculative markets like **SpaceX (SPCX)** and **Cerebras (CBRS)**.

### 4. Comparison: Trade.xyz vs. Traditional Brokers
Trade.xyz offers a "gapless" trading experience that traditional brokers like Robinhood or Interactive Brokers cannot match.

| Feature | Trade.xyz (Hyperliquid) | Traditional Brokers (e.g., Robinhood) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Trading Hours** | **True 24/7/365** | 24/5 (Closed Weekends/Holidays) |
| **Settlement** | **Instant (<1 second)** | T+1 (Standard) |
| **Leverage** | Up to **20x** on Indices | Typically 2x - 4x |
| **Transparency** | Fully On-Chain Order Book | Dark Pools / Internalized Flow |
| **Access** | Permissionless (Global) | Region-locked / Strict KYC |

**The 24/7 Advantage:** Traditional "24-hour" retail trading relies on private ATS (Alternative Trading Systems) which have thin liquidity and close on weekends. Trade.xyz uses an **8-hour EMA Oracle** to smooth price discovery during hours when the NYSE/CME are closed. This allows traders to react to global news on a Sunday morning that wouldn't be reflected in traditional markets until the Monday morning "gap" open.
