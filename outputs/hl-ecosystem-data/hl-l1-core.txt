Hyperliquid has evolved into a high-performance Layer 1 blockchain specifically engineered for low-latency financial applications. Below is the complete technical and operational breakdown of the network as of mid-2026.

### **Core Performance Metrics**
Hyperliquid utilizes a custom dual-engine architecture that separates the high-speed trading engine (**HyperCore**) from the general-purpose smart contract layer (**HyperEVM**).

*   **TPS (Transactions Per Second):**
    *   **HyperCore:** Capable of processing up to **200,000 orders per second**.
    *   **HyperEVM:** Optimized for financial logic, with theoretical scalability projected toward **1 million TPS** as execution optimizations continue.
*   **Block Time:** 
    *   Core trading actions achieve block times of **0.1 to 0.2 seconds**.
    *   **HyperEVM** employs a "dual block" system: "small blocks" every **1 second** for standard transactions and "large blocks" every **60 seconds** for heavy logic like contract deployments.
*   **Finality:** Sub-second finality is the standard. Median finality is approximately **0.2s**, with the 99th percentile under **0.9s**.
*   **Total Transactions:** The HyperEVM layer has processed approximately **138.5 million transactions** all-time. Cumulative trading volume on the L1 has surpassed **$4.4 trillion**.

### **Consensus & Security (HyperBFT)**
Hyperliquid does not use standard Tendermint or HotStuff; it uses **HyperBFT**, a custom consensus algorithm built from the ground up for trading.

*   **Mechanism:** An optimized BFT algorithm inspired by HotStuff but with a decoupled consensus and networking stack. This allows the network to stay "ahead" of the execution, minimizing the latency between a trade being sent and its inclusion in a block.
*   **Quorum:** Requires a **>2/3 majority** of the total stake to finalize a block.
*   **Validator Count:** Currently **25–30 active validators**. The network is gradually expanding toward a target of **50+ validators** by the end of 2026 to increase decentralization.

### **Staking & Tokenomics (HYPE)**
Staking is the primary security mechanism for the L1, with the native **HYPE** token serving as the stake.

*   **Total Staked:** Approximately **435 million HYPE**.
*   **Validator Requirements:** A minimum self-delegation of **10,000 HYPE** is required to join the active set.
*   **Staking Yield:** Currently ranges between **2.2% – 2.5% APR**.
*   **Unstaking Period:** Tokens are subject to a **7-day withdrawal queue**, providing a balance between security and capital efficiency.
