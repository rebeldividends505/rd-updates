Hyperliquid **HIP-4** (Hyperliquid Improvement Proposal 4), launched on mainnet on **May 2, 2026**, is a major upgrade that introduces **Outcome Trading** (native prediction markets and binary options) to the Hyperliquid Layer 1 blockchain.

### **1. Main Builders of HIP-4**
The development of HIP-4 was a collaborative effort between the core protocol team and strategic ecosystem partners:

*   **Hyperliquid Labs (Core Team):** Led by **Jeff Yan**, the core team designed the HyperCore infrastructure and the high-performance Central Limit Order Book (CLOB) that enables outcome contracts to be traded with the same speed and efficiency as perpetuals.
*   **Outcomexyz:** The primary builder of the user-facing application layer for HIP-4. They focus on market creation, UI/UX, and establishing the "HyperOutcome" ecosystem.
*   **Community Architects:** The proposal was influenced by research and momentum from **Framework Ventures**, **Kalshi** (specifically John Wang), and **Bedlam Research**, who were key proponents of bringing fully collateralized event contracts to the platform.

### **2. What is Outcomexyz?**
**Outcomexyz (Outcome.xyz)** is the flagship prediction market frontend built on the HIP-4 primitive. It functions similarly to Polymarket but is natively integrated into the Hyperliquid L1.

*   **Native Integration:** Unlike other prediction markets, Outcomexyz shares the same account, margin, and collateral pool as Hyperliquid’s perp exchange. You can use your perp collateral to buy "Outcome" contracts without moving funds.
*   **No Liquidation Risk:** Outcome contracts are fully collateralized, meaning they always trade between $0 and $1 and cannot be liquidated.
*   **Zero-Fee Model:** At launch, it introduced a zero-fee-to-open structure to attract liquidity and compete with established players.
*   **Primary Use Case:** It launched with daily Bitcoin price predictions and has expanded into macroeconomic events (Fed rates) and real-world outcomes.

### **Key Takeaways**
*   **Outcomexyz** is the "Polymarket of Hyperliquid," but with the advantage of being built into a professional-grade trading engine.
*   **Polymarket** remains the global volume leader by a significant margin, driven by its established user base on Polygon.
*   **HIP-4** represents a shift toward "Outcome Trading" as a core financial primitive, allowing traders to hedge perpetual positions with non-liquidatable binary options in a single click.
