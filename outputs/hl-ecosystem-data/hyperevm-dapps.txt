As of May 2026, the **Hyperliquid HyperEVM** ecosystem has matured into a multi-billion dollar Layer 1 environment. The ecosystem is uniquely characterized by its "precompile" architecture, which allows dApps on the EVM side to interact directly with the high-performance native perpetual order book (HyperCore).

Total ecosystem TVL currently fluctuates around **$4.5B – $5.0B**, with approximately **1.4 million unique addresses** and **247,000 daily active users (DAU)**.

### **Ecosystem Highlights & Metrics**
*   **HyperEVM Maturity:** The ecosystem is in its "Post-Alpha Mainnet" stage. The **CoreWriter** upgrade (July 2025) is the backbone of these dApps, allowing them to execute trades on the order book with sub-second finality.
*   **HYPE Token:** The native asset is trading between **$41.00 and $47.00** with a market cap of approximately **$11B**.
*   **User Growth:** Activity is driven by **HIP-3 (Permissionless Markets)** and **HIP-4 (Prediction Markets)**. These protocols have transitioned Hyperliquid from a crypto-only perp DEX into a global trading layer for traditional finance (TradFi) assets like BABA, TSLA, and Commodities.
*   **Dominant Strategy:** The most popular "money lego" involves staking HYPE on **Kinetiq** to get **kHYPE**, then depositing that kHYPE into **Felix** to mint **USDH**, which is then used to trade spot or provide liquidity on **Hyperswap**.

**Data Note:** TVL figures are estimates based on integrated on-chain state and protocol-reported metrics as of mid-2026. User counts refer to unique signing addresses interacting with HyperEVM contracts.
