### **Trajectory Analysis**
Hyperliquid's trajectory is characterized by **vertical scaling**:
1.  **Ecosystem Expansion:** It has successfully transitioned from a standalone DEX to a dedicated **Layer 1 blockchain**, which has reduced latency and allowed for the launch of native tokens like **HYPE**.

3.  **Network Effects:** As liquidity has deepened, it has become the primary venue for price discovery for many long-tail assets, further cementing its lead over other perp DEXes.
