Today's reading is higher than the 1929 peak. It is higher than 2007. It is higher than 2021. Every prior reading near 39 has been followed by a multi-year drawdown in the index. Not a prediction, a track record.
Warning Sign 2: Concentration
The AI Big 10 Are 40% of the S&P 500. That's Every Bubble Top Ever Recorded.
The Magnificent Seven plus Broadcom, Micron, and AMD now make up 40% of the entire S&P 500 by weight. The dot-com peak was 41%. Japan's Nikkei peak was 44%. The Nifty Fifty era topped near 40%. This is the most one-sided bet in the market today.

Capex Is Exploding. Can Earnings Keep Up?
Hyperscaler Capex Is Now Pulling Away From S&P 500 Earnings Growth.
For a decade, capex and earnings grew roughly together. Starting in 2023, the lines split. Capex is going vertical. Earnings growth has not kept pace. If revenue from AI products misses expectations even modestly, the entire chain unwinds.
