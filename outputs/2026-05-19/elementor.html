The Bear Case • What Would Prove Us Wrong
We’re conviction-loaded, not blind. Here’s what would change our view.
1. The rotation doesn’t happen. Capital sits in cash, or finds a new sector (biotech, energy re-acceleration). BTC stays range-bound at $70-80K. RD reinvestors collect dividends at a flat share price. Not catastrophic, but not the setup we’re describing.
