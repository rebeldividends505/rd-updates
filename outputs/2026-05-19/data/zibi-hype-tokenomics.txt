Based on data from the Hyperliquid protocol and current market metrics as of May 17, 2026, here is the status of the **HYPE** token buyback-and-burn mechanism, supply, and staking.

### **1. Buyback and Burn Status**
Hyperliquid utilizes an automated "Auto-Fill" (AF) mechanism that uses protocol revenue to buy back HYPE from the open market and permanently burn it.

*   **Total Burned:** Approximately **45.12 million HYPE** (roughly **14.5%** of the initial supply).
*   **Burn Mechanism:** 
    *   **Trading Fees:** ~97–100% of all perpetual and spot trading fees are directed toward buybacks.
    *   **L1 Gas Fees:** 100% of HYPE used for gas on the HyperEVM Layer 1 is burned.
    *   **Daily Burn Rate:** Currently averaging between **$824,000 and $1.5 million** worth of HYPE burned daily, fueled by nearly **$1 billion** in annual protocol revenue.

### **3. Staking Statistics**
HYPE staking secures the HyperCore (Layer 1) through a Delegated Proof-of-Stake (DPoS) model.

*   **Staking APY:** Estimated at **2.25% – 2.30%**.
*   **Total Tokens Staked:** ~114.9 million HYPE.
*   **Staking Ratio:** ~45.15% of the eligible supply is currently staked.
*   **Reward Source:** Rewards are paid from a dedicated **Future Emissions Reserve**, ensuring that trading fees can remain 100% dedicated to the deflationary burn mechanism.
*   **Unstaking Period:** There is a **1-day** period to unstake from a validator, followed by an additional **~7-day** withdrawal window to move funds back to a spot account.

### **Summary of Ecosystem Health**
The "HYPE" ecosystem currently holds over **$5.49 Billion** in Total Value Locked (TVL) across its bridge, HLP vault, and spot orderbook. The aggressive buyback mechanism effectively makes HYPE a deflationary asset relative to protocol growth, with the burn rate scaling directly with trading volume.
