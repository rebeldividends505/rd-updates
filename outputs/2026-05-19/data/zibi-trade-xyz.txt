trade.xyz (often stylized as **trade[XYZ]**) is the flagship decentralized exchange for **Real World Assets (RWAs)** built on the **Hyperliquid** Layer 1 blockchain. It utilizes the **HIP-3** (Hyperliquid Improvement Proposal 3) framework, which allows third-party developers to launch their own perpetual markets on top of Hyperliquid's existing high-performance matching engine.

While Hyperliquid's native platform focuses on crypto assets (BTC, ETH, etc.), trade.xyz focuses on **tokenized traditional assets**, allowing users to trade perpetual futures for equities (Nvidia, Tesla), commodities (Gold, Silver), and major indices with up to 50x leverage, settled in USDC.

### **How it fits in the Ecosystem**
Trade.xyz plays a critical role in the "Hyperliquid as a Layer 1" narrative by expanding the network's utility beyond crypto-native trading.

1.  **The "Traditional Finance" Bridge:** It allows DeFi users to hedge or speculate on the stock market and commodities without leaving the Hyperliquid ecosystem.
2.  **24/7 Trading:** Unlike traditional brokerage accounts (Robinhood, Charles Schwab), trade.xyz offers **24/7/365 liquidity** for assets like the S&P 500 and Nasdaq, which has become a major draw for global traders.
3.  **HIP-3 Flagship:** It serves as the primary proof-of-concept for Hyperliquid's permissionless market creation. It operates with its own fee structure and front-end but shares the underlying liquidity and security of the Hyperliquid L1.
4.  **Revenue Sharing:** Revenue generated by trade.xyz is typically split 50/50 between the trade.xyz team and the Hyperliquid protocol (which benefits HLP liquidity providers and the network), creating a symbiotic relationship between the builder and the base layer.
