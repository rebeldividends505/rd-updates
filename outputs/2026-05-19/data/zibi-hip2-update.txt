### **Recent Launches & Narrative Shifts (May 2026)**
The "Mega Month" of May 2026 introduced several high-impact developments:

*   **Spot HYPE ETFs ($THYP, $BHYP):** 21Shares and Bitwise launched the first U.S. spot ETFs for the HYPE token. Notably, the Bitwise ETF includes **native staking rewards**, a first for U.S. institutional crypto products.
*   **HIP-4 Outcome Markets:** Launched on May 2nd, this prediction market feature allows trading on real-world events (like BTC price direction) using unified cross-margin from spot and perp accounts.
*   **The USDC Pivot:** Circle and Coinbase have officially partnered with Hyperliquid to establish **USDC as the Aligned Quote Asset (AQA)**. This has effectively phased out the older USDH stablecoin in favor of institutional-grade USDC liquidity.
*   **SOVRN (Sovrun):** A major new ecosystem project that listed in mid-May and quickly rose into the top volume rankings.
