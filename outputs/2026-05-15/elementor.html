Method notes — what the backtest does and does not claim

Window. 52 weeks ending 2026-05-17. HYPE only launched in late 2024, so a longer window is impossible without a synthetic proxy — we chose to be honest about the horizon instead.

Data. Equities (QQQ, SOXL) via yfinance weekly bars. Crypto (BTC, ETH, HYPE) via CoinGecko weekly resampling. All returns are total return (auto-adjusted for equity dividends/splits).

Assumptions. Weekly rebalance at Sunday close. No fee model in the printed numbers (estimate 2-4% annual drag). No funding rate model (Hyperliquid funding is typically flat-to-positive on top assets). Leverage is gross notional — you'd need ~60% of your HYPE as margin for a 1.5x BTC + 0.5x ETH position at current vol.

What this is not. Not a guarantee. Not a forecast. Strategy B looked plausible on paper and lost 42% in 52 weeks — a reminder that "the rotation thesis" needs proof, not vibes. The point of this page is that we ran the numbers in public.
