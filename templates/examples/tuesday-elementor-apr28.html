Tax Treatment: Return of Capital treatment under IRC §301(c)(2) is available only in years where corporate distributions exceed current and accumulated earnings and profits; classification can change year-to-year based on corporate performance. Return-of-Capital distributions reduce a shareholder's adjusted tax basis in their stock; any distribution in excess of adjusted basis is taxed as capital gain. Rebel Dividends Corporation has classified 2026 distributions as Return of Capital; shareholders will receive a corrected 1099-DIV reflecting Box 3 treatment. Consult your CPA regarding how this classification applies to your individual circumstances.
